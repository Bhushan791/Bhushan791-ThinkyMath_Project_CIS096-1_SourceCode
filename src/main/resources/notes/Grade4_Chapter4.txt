Finding perimeter of shapes
Understanding area
✨ Perimeter = Sum of all sides! 🔄
Example: Rectangle (5 cm × 3 cm)
Perimeter = 5 + 3 + 5 + 3 = 16 cm

✨ Area = Length × Width 📐
Example: Rectangle (5 cm × 3 cm)
Area = 5 × 3 = 15 cm²

🚀 Tip: Square’s area formula: Side × Side 🎯
