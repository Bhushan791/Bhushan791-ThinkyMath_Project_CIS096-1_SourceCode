🍰 Chapter 4: Fractions
🎯 What will we learn?

Understanding fractions and how to read them
Identifying parts of a whole
✨ Fractions Examples:

1/2 (half) means 1 part out of 2 equal parts 🍕
1/3 (third) means 1 part out of 3 equal parts 🍰
1/4 (quarter) means 1 part out of 4 equal parts 🍩
✨ Drawing Fractions:
If you have a circle, and you divide it into 4 parts:

1/4 is one part of the 4 🍩
2/4 is two parts of the 4 🍩🍩
4/4 means the whole circle is filled up! 🥧
