✨ Fractions Examples:

1/2 = 🍕 Half of a pizza
1/4 = 🍩 One-quarter of a donut
✨ Equivalent Fractions:

1/2 = 2/4 = 3/6 🎯
1/3 = 2/6 = 3/9
🚀 Tip: If you multiply or divide the numerator & denominator by the same number, you get an equivalent fraction!
