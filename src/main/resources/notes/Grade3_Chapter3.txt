✖️ Chapter 3: Multiplication & Division
🎯 What will we learn?

Exploring multiplication and division
Understanding times tables and dividing into equal parts
✨ Multiplication Example:

34 × 2 = 68
Think of it as 34 repeated 2 times: 34 + 34 = 68. 📏
✨ Division Example:

72 ÷ 8 = 9
If you have 72 objects and divide them into 8 groups, each group will have 9 objects. 🍎🍎🍎🍎🍎🍎🍎🍎🍎
