📏 Chapter 5: Measurement (Length, Weight, Capacity)
🎯 What will we learn?

Measuring length, weight, and capacity
Understanding how to measure everyday things
✨ Length:

Short (✂️) vs. long (📏)
Example: A pencil is short 🖊️, and a ruler is long 📐
✨ Weight:

Light (🪶) vs. heavy (🏋️‍♀️)
Example: A feather is light 🪶, and a dumbbell is heavy 🏋️‍♀️
✨ Capacity:

Small capacity (🥛 small cup) vs. large capacity (🍼 large bottle)
Example: A cup holds less water 🥄, while a bottle holds more water 🧃
