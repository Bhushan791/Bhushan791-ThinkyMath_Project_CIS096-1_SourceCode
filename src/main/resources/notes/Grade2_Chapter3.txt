🧠 Chapter 3: Mental Arithmetic
🎯 What will we learn?

Quick addition and subtraction without writing it down
Using mental math strategies to solve problems fast!
✨ Addition Example:

12 + 7 = 19
You can think: “12 + 8 = 20, then subtract 1. So, 19!” 🧠✨
✨ Subtraction Example:

25 - 9 = 16
You can think: “25 - 10 = 15, then add 1. So, 16!” 🔢🤓
