Reading clock and calendar
✨ Reading Clocks:

Analog Clock:
The clock has 2 hands: the hour hand (short) and the minute hand (long). 🕒
If the hour hand is on 3 and the minute hand is on 12, it’s 3:00.
✨ Reading Calendars:

Months of the Year:
There are 12 months in a year:
January, February, March... December 🎆🎉
30 days in April, June, September, and November.
31 days in the other months except February.
February has 28 or 29 days depending on the year! 🎃
